Aqua Pure Website

Descption

As a group, our main objective is to create the website "AquaPure," which complies with Goal 6 of the Sustainable Development Goals (Clean Water and Sanitation) and some of Goal 14's requirements (Life Below Water). Moreover, our major objective is to increase public awareness so that more people will realise the need of water filtration and take action to do so. This will hopefully achieve our aim of clean water and improve the convenience of living below the surface.
