The Problem

Sewage and agricultural pollution still plague the rivers and ocean. In 2020 there were over 400,000 discharges of untreated sewage into the UK rivers and almost 5,500 discharges into the UK coastal bathing waters

The sheer volume of sewage and run-off entering the water means the UK is consistently ranked as one of the worst European countries for coastal water quality and only 14% of rivers meet good ecological status.
